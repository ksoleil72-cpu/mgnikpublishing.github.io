SMS Notice

SMS messaging policy & consent for MGNik Publishing

About

MGNik Publishing is an independent book publishing company. SMS messaging is used on a limited basis for direct, low-volume communication with the business owner and authorized contacts only. This is not a public subscription service.

What messages are sent

Authorized contacts may receive the following types of SMS messages:

  • Scheduling reminders and shift updates
  • Operational status alerts
  • Project and workflow updates
  • General business-related communication

Messages are not used for marketing, advertising, or promotional purposes.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies. Typical usage is 1–3 messages per day, with a maximum of 10 messages per day. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt out

Reply STOP to any message to opt out at any time. Upon receipt of STOP, no further messages will be sent. Opting out of SMS will not affect communications delivered through other channels.

Help

Reply HELP for assistance, or contact dahlia@mgnikpublishing.com.

Privacy

Mobile phone numbers and personal information will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. SMS opt-in data and consent records will not be shared with any third parties.
