SMS Notice

SMS messaging policy for MGNik Publishing

About

MGNik Publishing is an independent book publishing company. SMS messaging is used on a limited basis for direct, low-volume communication with the business owner and authorized contacts only.

What messages are sent

Messages may include scheduling reminders, project updates, status alerts, and general business-related communication. This is a low-volume use case. Messages are not used for marketing, advertising, or promotional purposes.

Message frequency

Messages are limited to no more than 10 per day. Typical usage is 1–3 messages per day.

How consent is obtained

SMS consent is obtained directly by the business owner during account setup. The business owner personally authorizes their own phone number to receive messages. No public opt-in form is used because messages are sent only to the business owner and individually authorized contacts who provide written consent prior to receiving any messages.

Opt out

Reply STOP to any message to opt out at any time. Upon receipt of STOP, no further messages will be sent.

Help

Reply HELP for assistance, or contact dahlia@mgnikpublishing.com.

Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan.

Privacy

Mobile phone numbers and personal information will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. SMS opt-in data and consent records will not be shared with any third parties.
