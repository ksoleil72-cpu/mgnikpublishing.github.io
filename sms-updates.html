MGNik Publishing does not offer a public SMS signup program on this website. This page is provided only to explain how SMS messaging is handled for authorized contacts.

Opt out: Reply STOP to opt out at any time.

Help: Reply HELP for help or contact dahlia@mgnikpublishing.com.

This page is a compliance notice, not a public subscription form. If a carrier or provider requests evidence of opt-in, the business should provide its internal consent records or screenshots of the actual non-public consent method used.
